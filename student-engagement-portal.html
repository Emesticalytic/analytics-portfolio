Business Analysis Project

Student Engagement Portal

A digital service improvement project focused on improving student communication, engagement, support access and operational visibility across an education setting.

Business Problem

Student communication and support processes were fragmented across manual channels, making it difficult for staff to track engagement, identify students needing support and provide timely updates to students and parents.

Objective

Design a user-centred engagement portal that would centralise communication, support requests, student updates and engagement tracking while reducing manual administration for staff.

My BA Contribution

  • Facilitated workshops with senior leaders, academic staff, admin users and student-facing teams.
  • Mapped current and future processes to identify handover gaps, duplicated effort and reporting delays.
  • Produced stakeholder analysis, user needs, epics, user stories, acceptance criteria and backlog items.
  • Supported backlog refinement, sprint planning, UAT preparation, training materials and change adoption.
  • Maintained project documentation and used RAID/RAG thinking to monitor risks, blockers and dependencies.

Example Epic

Epic: Student Communication and Support Visibility

As a student support officer, I need a single view of engagement updates and support requests so that I can identify students who may need intervention earlier.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Staff can view student engagement status from a central dashboard.
  • Support requests can be logged, categorised and assigned to the right owner.
  • Users can filter by year group, status, priority and assigned staff member.
  • Reports show overdue actions, open cases and resolved support requests.

Process Flow

Student update Support request Staff triage Action assigned Outcome tracked

MVP Scope

  • Student profile and engagement status.
  • Support request logging and ownership.
  • Basic reporting dashboard.
  • User guidance and training materials.

Outcome

The project improved visibility of student engagement, reduced fragmented manual communication and gave staff a clearer process for identifying and responding to support needs.
